History

Zona Rafting was established in 2010 with the goal of providing thrilling rafting experiences. Over the years, we have grown to become a leading rafting guide service, offering unforgettable adventures in some of the most beautiful rivers.

Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on every trip. We believe in responsible tourism and strive to protect the natural environment while providing exciting experiences for our guests.
